Prism KPI Dashboard & Revenue Analysis

This dashboard provides an overview of Prism’s overall business performance, combining key KPIs with trends in customer behaviour and revenue. While total revenue (£8.7M) and gross profit margin (47.7%) indicate strong financial performance, the low conversion rate (0.59%) and declining retention trend highlight opportunities to improve customer acquisition and loyalty. Revenue shows fluctuations throughout the year but peaks towards the end of 2021, suggesting seasonal growth and stronger performance in later months.

Profitability & Revenue Performance Overview

This dashboard highlights key revenue and profitability trends across brands, genders, and time periods. It shows that women’s products consistently generate higher profits for top brands like Tommy Hilfiger and U.S. Polo Assn., while overall revenue demonstrates strong growth—peaking in Q4. Year-on-year performance also improved, with 2021 outperforming 2020, indicating positive business momentum and seasonal strength in later quarters.
